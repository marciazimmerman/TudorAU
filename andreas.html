Andreas Cramer Berg

Andreas Cramer Berg was Henry's fourth spouse and first husband. It is widely believed that they divorced after Henry's experimentation with a same-sex marriage ran its course.

Origin

Andreas was born in Germany.

Issue

Andreas and Henry had no children.

His relationship with Henry

Andreas cared a great deal for Henry, but was equally intrigued by the idea of being husband of such a powerful man. When Henry's eye turned to the young and foolish Caitee Howard, Andreas was only too happy to let Henry go.
