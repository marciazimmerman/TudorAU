Catherine "Cath" Parr

Cath Parr was Henry's sixth spouse and fifth wife.

Origin

Cath was born in Los Angeles, California.

Professional life

She is a software developer turned yoga teacher, and saw a marriage to Henry as a way to inject stillness and mindfulness into his corporate culture.

Family ties

Cath bore Henry no children. She was close - closer than he was, in fact - to her putative stepdaughters Mary and Lizbee, and to her stepson Wardo.
